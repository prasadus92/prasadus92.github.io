The Problem

B2B companies spend billions on events every year. Trade shows, conferences, executive dinners, roadshows - the list goes on. For most companies, events represent 20-30% of their entire marketing budget.

Yet when CFOs ask "what's the ROI?", most marketing teams struggle to answer. The data is scattered across spreadsheets, CRMs, and email threads. Attendee lists come in late. Sales teams scramble to book meetings. Post-event follow-up is messy and untracked.

The result? 40-70% of event-generated leads disappear before they ever make it to a sales conversation.
